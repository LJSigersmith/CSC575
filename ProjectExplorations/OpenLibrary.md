
## 8. Open Library
### Repository
> [openlibrary repo](https://github.com/internetarchive/openlibrary)

### Homepage
> [openlibrary homepage](https://openlibrary.org/)

### Product
> A digital library with the goal of one web page for every book ever published. It collects millions of book records and has a wiki interface. "The ultimate goal of the Open Library is to make all the published works of humankind available to everyone in the world."

### Target User Community
> Researchers, readers, academics.

### Activity
