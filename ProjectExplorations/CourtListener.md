## 9. Court Listener (Free Law Project)

### Repository
> [CourtListener Repo](https://github.com/freelawproject/courtlistener)

### Homepage
> [CourtListener Homepage](https://www.courtlistener.com/)

### Product
> Searchable archive of court data: opinions, oral arguments, judges, judicial financial records, federal filings. The Free Law Project started in 2010 to make the legal ecosystem more equitable and competitive. The archive aims to provide free, public, and permananet access to primary legal materials.

### Target User Community
> Lawyers, journalists, academics, the public.
